About Us

At this time programmers are in demand in the world of work, therefore GROW provides programming language courses with online-based learning methods, for participants who want to learn a programming language, but are constrained by time and distance so that the material can be accessed anytime and anywhere. The presentation of the material is carried out by qualified instructors and experts in their fields. GROW can solve problems that are usually experienced by a programmer as bellow :

You are looking for a job but haven't experience
You don't master perfectly one of the programming languages
You want to add a portofolio to apply for a job
Improve your skills with experience working on industrial-scale projects and you can include it in your CV as work experience.
